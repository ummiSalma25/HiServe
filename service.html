How to Get Started?

Interested in studying abroad with HiServe Global Education? Here are the steps you need to follow.

Contact us to book a session with one of our counselors. You can call us, send us an email, or visit our office near you. Our counselor will assess your eligibility and suggest the best course and country for you, based on your academic background, financial situation, and career aspirations.

Our counselors will help you with the application process and requirements, and guide you on how to write a strong statement of purpose, resume, and recommendation letters. We will ensure that your application is error-free, and that it meets the deadlines and standards of the university.

Once you receive your admission offer, accept it and pay the tuition deposit to secure your placement. We will help you with the payment process and receipt.

Apply for your student visa, with our help and guidance. We will help you prepare your visa application and documents, and advise you on how to face the visa interview, if required. We will also assist you with the visa fee payment and appointment booking.

Once you've recived your visa approval and confirmation, we will prepare you for your departure by helping you with your travel arrangements, such as flight booking, accommodation, and insurance. We will wish you all the best, and stay in touch with you throughout your course.
